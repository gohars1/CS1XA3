About Me




When I first entered University, I never thought I'd be a Programmer. I was originally enrolled in McMaster's Kinesiology program. I've always had an interest in technology, however, my interest in health and fitness led me to choose to pursue Kinesiology. Although I did enjoy Kinesiology, I began to learn and become more interested in the ubiquitous nature of programming. The summer after my first year, I applied for a transfer to Computer Science at McMaster University. Very quickly, I began volunteering with Mac Outreach to improve technical literacy in youth and participated in a Hackathon where my team earned the RBC sponsorship award. I am a passionate individual and I have brought this drive to my pursuit of becoming a Computer Scientist. Outside of technology, I am interested in the outdoors, sports (particularly basketball, martial arts, and weightlifting), music and trying new things.
